About Us

Over 60 Years of Garment Manufacturing Expertise

Siva Vel Trends (SVT) is a globally recognized textile and garment manufacturing company with over 60 years of experience. We are committed to delivering high-quality fashion garments through a vertically integrated setup that spans yarn production to finished product delivery.

  • Producing 300,000+ garments every month
  • Global exports with strict quality inspections
  • 60% of our employees are women, promoting inclusive growth
